# Rondel

Can move 3 places at no cost, every additional place costs 1 + Power Factor on scoring track in million. Maximum move is 6 places. 

CalculateMoveOpportunity rondel nation nationPowerFactor -> (position, fee) list

where
type Rondel = (Nation & Position) list
and type Position = Investor | Import | ... | Manuver
